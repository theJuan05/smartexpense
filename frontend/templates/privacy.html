Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026  ·  SmartExpense

Who we are

SmartExpense is a personal expense tracking app built for individual use. It is a student project developed to help users manage their daily finances.

If you have any questions about this policy, email us at amiguelll0513@gmail.com.

What data we collect and why

We only collect what the app needs to function. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Data Why we collect it Stored how
Name & email address Account creation and login Plain text (email is your identifier)
Password Authentication Hashed with bcrypt — never stored in plaintext
Expense title & notes Tracking your spending Encrypted with AES-256-GCM before saving to the database
Expense amounts, dates, categories Budget analysis and reports Plain text in database
Profile photo Profile display Stored as base64 in your account record
Device push token Budget alert notifications Stored in database, sent to Firebase FCM
IP address & action timestamps Security audit log (login, logout, registration) Stored in audit_logs table

Third-party services we use

SmartExpense relies on the following external services. Each has its own privacy policy.

  • Aiven (MySQL) — our database host. Your data lives on Aiven-managed servers in the cloud.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — used to deliver push notifications to your device. Your push token is shared with Firebase when you enable notifications.
  • Google Gemini AI — used to generate spending advice and receipt scan results. Expense data is sent to Google's API to generate these responses.
  • Brevo (Sendinblue) — used to send verification emails and password reset emails.
  • AWS EC2 — our server host. The app runs on an AWS instance in the ap-southeast-2 (Sydney) region.

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

How we protect your data

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt before storage. We never see your actual password and cannot recover it — only reset it.

Expense titles and notes are encrypted with AES-256-GCM before being written to the database. The encryption key is derived server-side from a secret stored in the environment — it is not in the database. Each value is encrypted with a unique random nonce.

All connections between your browser and our server use HTTPS (TLS), enforced by a Let's Encrypt certificate. Plain HTTP requests are automatically redirected.

Database queries use parameterized statements throughout the codebase — user input is never concatenated directly into SQL.

Your rights

  • Access your data — everything you've entered is visible in the app.
  • Delete individual records — you can delete any expense at any time.
  • Delete your account — going to Settings → Delete Account permanently removes your user record, all expenses, budgets, goals, and push tokens from our database.
  • Export your data — use the Export button in the app to download your expenses as a CSV or PDF.

Cookies and sessions

SmartExpense uses a single server-side session cookie to keep you logged in for up to 30 days. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party cookies.

Data retention

Your data is kept for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, all associated data is deleted immediately and cannot be recovered. We do not keep backups of deleted accounts.
