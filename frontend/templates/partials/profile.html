Privacy & Security

Your data is yours. SmartExpense stores your expenses securely in an encrypted cloud database. Only you can access your account.

What we store: Expense records, budget limits, and account credentials. Expense titles and notes are encrypted at rest using AES-256.

What we never do: We never sell your data, share it with advertisers, or access it without your permission.

Passwords: Your password is hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text. Change it anytime in Settings.

Backups: Use the Backup Data feature in Settings to keep a local copy of your expenses at any time.
